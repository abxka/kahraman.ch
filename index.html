About

Where deep science meets clinical practice.

Kahraman Life and Data Science Consulting is a boutique advisory firm led by Prof. Dr. Abdullah Kahraman — a computational biologist and data scientist with a career spanning the European Bioinformatics Institute at Cambridge, ETH Zurich, the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics, University Hospital Zurich, University Hospital Basel and FHNW.

We bridge the gap between cutting-edge computational research and the practical realities of clinical and industrial life science settings — bringing world-class scientific rigour and Swiss precision to every engagement.

High demand · DACH hospitals

Molecular Tumor Board Support

Precision oncology implementation remains unstandardized across DACH institutions. We provide NGS data interpretation, MTPpilot-based visualization, and bioinformatics support to establish and streamline Molecular Tumor Boards at hospitals and cancer centres.

  • NGS interpretation
  • MTPpilot
  • Clinical workflows
  • Precision oncology

Fast-growing · Pharma & diagnostics

Liquid Biopsy & Multi-Analyte Profiling

The liquid biopsy market is growing at ~12% CAGR and DACH pharma companies are investing heavily in non-invasive early detection. We develop and validate multimodal algorithms integrating cfDNA, cfRNA, and plasma proteomics for pan-cancer and organ-specific early detection and monitoring.

  • cfDNA / cfRNA
  • Plasma proteomics
  • Multi-analyte
  • Early detection

AI & ML for Drug Discovery

Swiss and German pharma companies are investing hundreds of millions into AI-driven discovery. We build and validate ML/DL models for biomarker identification, treatment response prediction, and neoantigen pipelines — bridging computational biology with clinical data.

  • ML / DL
  • Biomarker AI
  • Neoantigen
  • Drug discovery

Cancer Genomics & NGS Pipelines

End-to-end design and implementation of NGS analysis pipelines — WGS, WES, RNA-seq, and long-read sequencing. Tumor mutational landscape interpretation, actionable variant identification, and multi-omics integration for research and clinical teams.

  • WGS / WES
  • RNA-seq
  • Long-read
  • Multi-omics

Emerging · Biomarker & diagnostics companies

Alternative Splicing & Isoform Biomarkers

Cancer-specific alternative splicing is widely disrupted but underexploited diagnostically. We detect isoform switching events, assess their functional impact via CanIsoNet, and develop splicing-based biomarker signatures for diagnostic and therapeutic applications.

  • Isoform switching
  • CanIsoNet
  • Biomarker dev.
  • Long-read RNA

Clinical Data Platform & Infrastructure

Hospitals and clinical research networks need FHIR-compatible bioinformatics infrastructure to handle increasing NGS panel complexity. We design data platforms, omics databases, and interactive dashboards that integrate into existing hospital IT environments.

  • Dashboards
